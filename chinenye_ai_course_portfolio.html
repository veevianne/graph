About the course

Responsible AI for real work.

Generative AI for Work & Research is an 8-week live cohort course by The GRAPH Courses focused on no-code AI tools for responsible task automation. The course includes weekly live workshops, help sessions, private community forums, personalized feedback, capstone coaching, a shareable certificate, and lifetime access to a tutorial library.
